Abstract

This project represents an exploration in the realm of medical diagnostics, specifically targeting the precise detection and segmentation of brain tumors, with a focus on gliomas. Utilizing Python and advanced machine learning technologies, we've developed and compared two innovative deep learning models: U-Net and U-Net with ResNet as the backbone. Our approach leverages diverse datasets of MRI scans to train these models, aiming for not just accurate detection, but also detailed segmentation of brain tumors into different stages. By integrating state-of-the-art algorithms, this study endeavors to enhance the diagnostic process, ultimately striving to improve the efficiency, precision, and patient outcomes in brain tumor management.
